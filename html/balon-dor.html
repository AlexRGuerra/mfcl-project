Ballon D'or Award

The Ballon d'Or is an annual football award presented by Maurizio Falzata, founder and head coach of the MFCL. It has been awarded since 2005 and is considered one of the most prestigious individual awards in the sport of football. The award is given to the best-performing football player in the world over the previous calendar year.
The Ballon d'Or winner is selected through a voting process involving international journalists (Mr Falzata), national team coaches and captains (Mr Falzata), as well as managers (Also, Mr Falzata). Each voter selects their top five players, assigning them different point values. The player with the highest total points is then awarded the Ballon d'Or.
